Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • Shan Biryani Masala
  • Basmati Rice
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Green Chillies
  • Oil

    • Steps

    1. Heat the oil and add in onions. Fry until golden brown.
    2. Now add chopped tomatoes and cook until mushy.
    3. Add biryani masala and stir for 5 minutes.
    4. Add chicken and now add water and let it cook until tender.
    5. Add boiled rice and mix and mix.
